Walkthrough:
1. Change Jump Height to 30 to jump over the first two walls
2. Change Arm Angle to 0 to shoot straight
3. Drop down and kill the dude
4. Move right next to spikes
5. Change gravity scale to 0, jump and float above the spikes (but stop before the ceiling ends)
6. Change gravity back to 2 and change jump height to 20 and jump over the ledge without hitting the spikes
7. Change jump to 25, jump on the ledge and kill the two dudes
8. Move to finish line!

Alternativaly:
1. Change health to 500
2. Change jump height to 30 and breeze through this game like boss
